SERVICES

We will do our best

Construction

We provide a full construction service from inception to completion with an obsessive eye for quality. We offer a thorough and comprehensive approach that has enabled us to deliver projects to our Clients' satisfaction.

Consulting

SIDDIQUE AND SONS provides comprehensive construction consulting services. These services include, Supervising, Owner’s Representative and Building Condition Assessment. We provide expertise to stakeholders in a variety of projects.

Urban Designing

We create sustainable city environments and enduring places of beauty that transform our everyday lives. We work in places of magnificent urban opportunity that demand creativity in tackling growth, transformation and community passions. We integrate high-level landscape architecture into our urban design work. Whether it’s a prominent urban district or high-density living.

Interior Designing

We create dynamic, comprehensive spaces for a variety of functions ranging from hospitality, residential, retail and commercial. SIDDIQUE AND SONS is a supreme source for interior designing that provides the highest level of service in the industry. We offer full range of services including Bridal Room, Living Room, Dining Room, Bedroom, Kitchen and Bathroom Interiors.
